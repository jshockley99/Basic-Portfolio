About Me

My name is Julie Shockley, and I'm a web developer. I'm also passionate about beautiful design. Whether it's the code that runs behind the scenes or the visual layout, I strive to achieve simple elegance. My education is in Fine Arts, and I have spent my most of professional career in system development. Combining the two, I'm embarking on a career in web design and development.

In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, running, or working on one of the many projects around the house.
